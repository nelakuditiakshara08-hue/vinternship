# 17-02-2026  The Day It All Started

It was not a special day.

Just another evening, walking back from college, scrolling through emails without really paying attention. Some unread, some ignored.

And then, one email appeared.

At first, it did not feel like anything important. I opened it casually, still distracted, not fully aware of what I was reading. I did not realize what had just happened.

For a few seconds, I just stood there, staring at the screen. It did not sink in immediately. It felt unreal.

Almost instinctively, I called a friend who was doing her NPTEL course, just like me. I asked if she had received the same email. She said no.

And that is when it hit me.

**This was not just another email.**

---

## Where It Actually Began

Even after that moment, I was not completely sure.

A part of me was excited, but another part kept questioning—*Was this even real? Or just another spam email?*

I remember talking to my parents about it, still unsure of what to do. My father listened quietly and simply said:

> Give it a try.

That was it.

No overthinking. No pressure.

And that’s where this entire journey truly began.

---

## Key Takeaway

The journey remains consistent, even when growth doesn’t feel visible.

Effort doesn’t always reflect immediately in results.

But that doesn’t mean it isn’t shaping you.

> Sometimes, the most important changes are the ones you don’t notice right away.
